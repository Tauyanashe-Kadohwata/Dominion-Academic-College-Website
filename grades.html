Grades We Have

Our school consists of the following gardes:

Pre-School

Grade RRR: Babies ranging from 3 months-3 years
of age.
Grade RR: Kids ranging from 4-5 years of age.
Grade R: Kids ranging from 5-6 years of age.

Foundation Phase

Grade 1: Kids ranging from 6-7 years of age.
Grade 2: Kids ranging from 7-8 years of age.
Grade 3: Kids ranging from 8-9 years of age.

Intermediate Phase

Grade 4: Learners ranging from 9-10 years of age.
Grade 5: Learners ranging from 10-11 years of age.
Grade 6: Learners ranging from 11-12 years of age.

Senior Phase

Grade 7: Learners ranging from 12-13 years of age.

Step By Step Process Of Enrolment And Requirements

Requirements:

Parents/Guardians legal documents.
Transfer card from learner's previous school.
Report card from the learner's previous school.
Health Documents of the learner.
Passport photos of the learner.
Copy of learner's birth certificate.
Copy of learner's graduation certificate.(Applies only to Grade 1 enrolments)

Steps to enroll

*Come to any of our locations and inquire for an enrolment form.
*Fill in details required and submit necessary documents.
*Pay registration fee of R200 once-off
*Confirm when the child/children can start learning, and if you are proceeding,
you MUST pay school fees before we start providing our services. No negotiations applied.
