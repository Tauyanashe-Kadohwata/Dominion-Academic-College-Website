Our Enrollment Process 2025!!

Dominion Academic College Enrollment Process

This flyer serves you with more information about our enrollment process for our primary school
in 2025 and gives you extra information regarding the extra mural activities we will have throughout the year.

Our school fees this year will be R750 per month x 12 months.
It can also be paid in annual installment which will cost approx. R9000.
When enrolling for the first time, it is compulsory to pay registration fees, which
will be R200 once off. Stationery Fees is compulsory to be paid for every learner. We DO NOT accept learners to have their own purchased stationery.
Stationery fees is mandatory because everything will be provided for the learner, e.g. pens, pencils, writing books and even toner for printers.
Stationery fees costs R1000 per learner and can either be paid once off, or in 2 or 4 installments. We also have promotional discounts for learners who are siblings, as well as referral discounts e.g. you refer a friend to enrol their child
and the enrollment is succesful, we call you in for a discount.

Beta-Child Pre-School Enrollment Process

Our pre-school's fees this year will be R750 per month x 12 months.
It can also be paid in annual installment which will cost approx. R9000.
When enrolling for the first time, it is compulsory to pay registration fees, which
will be R200 once off. Stationery Fees is compulsory to be paid for every learner. We DO NOT accept learners to have their own purchased stationery.
Stationery fees is mandatory because everything will be provided for the learner, e.g. pens, pencils, writing books and even toner for printers.
This only applies to Grade RR and R leaners, NOT babies and early toddlers(Grade RRR) Stationery fees costs R1000 per learner and can either be paid once off, or in 2 or 4 installments. We also have promotional discounts for learners who are siblings, as well as referral discounts e.g. you refer a friend to enrol their child
and the enrollment is succesful, we call you in for a discount. Every learner enrolling in our pre-school MUST bring a tub of Vaseline, a blanket, 3 packets of wet wipes, a ream of bond paper(only applies to Grade RR and R) and 6 toilet paper rolls.
